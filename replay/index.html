Replays —
a second try, studied.

Positions you replayed from a game you’d already finished — picking up from a critical moment to see how it could have gone. These are analyzed exactly like a normal game, but kept separate here on purpose: replays are excluded from the drill deck and the progress dashboard, so a position you revisit doesn’t double-count against your real games.

Why a replay is walled off. When you continue a lost game from a turning point, the same mistakes and positions would otherwise be counted twice — once in the original, once in the do-over — inflating your recurring-mistake tallies and drill schedule. Keeping replays in replay/ lets you learn from the second attempt without polluting the statistics that track your genuine games.
